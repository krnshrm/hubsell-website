<div class="kb-callout">
  <p>Connecting these two accounts is what lets hubsell send on your behalf. Your mailbox powers email steps, and the LinkedIn connection powers LinkedIn steps in your campaigns.</p>
</div>

<h2 class="kb-sub">Connect your mailbox</h2>
<div class="kb-callout kb-callout--tip">
  <p>hubsell supports <b>Google</b> and <b>Microsoft</b> mailboxes. The steps below show Google. Microsoft follows the same path from the Mailbox tab, using your Microsoft sign-in instead.</p>
</div>
<ol class="kb-steps">
  <li><p>Click your profile icon and choose <span class="kb-kbd">User Settings</span>.</p></li>
  <li>
    <p>Open the <span class="kb-kbd">Mailbox</span> tab.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: the Mailbox tab in User Settings">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-16/b98ae02d-fc84-4322-852e-9241462eecca/sps-redacted-2d0132c732214dbc98db7d12eeea4995_d800a5ccc5724165b910766d228abbba_text_export.jpeg" alt="The Mailbox tab under User Settings" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>The Mailbox tab under User Settings</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Choose your email provider. For a Google account, click <span class="kb-kbd">Sign in with Google</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: choosing the email provider">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-16/04f9f832-ab55-48ed-bf52-93a0ccbbf8db/sps-redacted-e9d83053ce224f7892d1c7df04e863c8_a2d0b304c166492bad2e43478b2d66a9_text_export.jpeg" alt="Choosing an email provider with Sign in with Google" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Choosing your email provider</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li><p>Select the account you want to connect.</p></li>
  <li><p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Continue</span>.</p></li>
  <li><p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Allow</span> to grant hubsell the permissions it needs to send and track mail.</p></li>
</ol>

<h2 class="kb-sub">Connect LinkedIn</h2>
<div class="kb-callout kb-callout--tip">
  <p>LinkedIn connects through a Chrome extension. You install it once, then link it to your LinkedIn account.</p>
</div>
<ol class="kb-steps" start="7">
  <li>
    <p>In the same User Settings menu, open the <span class="kb-kbd">LinkedIn</span> tab.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: the LinkedIn tab in User Settings">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-16/22479dec-a7bb-490f-b31a-8265b91c2697/sps-redacted-6f122f243de343e68200475d6367754a_1b364ddf2f934ff1b05e602bd1323021_text_export.jpeg" alt="The LinkedIn tab under User Settings" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>The LinkedIn tab under User Settings</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li><p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Download Google Chrome extension</span>.</p></li>
  <li><p>In the Chrome Web Store, click <span class="kb-kbd">Add to Chrome</span>.</p></li>
  <li><p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Add extension</span> to confirm the install.</p></li>
  <li><p>Open the hubsell plugin from your browser's Extensions menu.</p></li>
  <li>
    <p>In the plugin popup, click <span class="kb-kbd">Connect to LinkedIn</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: Connect to LinkedIn in the plugin popup">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-16/8d13a803-487b-4c03-8376-c69929423d8c/sps-redacted-e8cd9dfa3c8d4651bf04c384576347c3_ecc70bf95cc24fe09fd25f726746eb55_text_export.jpeg" alt="The Connect to LinkedIn button in the plugin popup" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Connecting LinkedIn from the plugin</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Refresh the page. The LinkedIn tab should now show as connected.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: LinkedIn showing as connected">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-16/6cf6d379-a096-4bb2-8a33-6de6c4a86001/screenshot_2ebc9cbdc0f0499da9d9ba8ff90aa5b9_text_export.jpeg" alt="The LinkedIn tab showing a connected status" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>LinkedIn connected</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
</ol>

  <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" aria-hidden="true"><path d="M22 11.08V12a10 10 0 1 1-5.93-9.14"/><path d="M22 4L12 14.01l-3-3"/></svg>

<div class="kb-callout kb-callout--warn">
  <p class="kb-callout__h">If LinkedIn stops working</p>
  <p>If the LinkedIn tab later shows as disconnected, or a search pauses on its own, your LinkedIn session has usually gone out of sync. Open the hubsell plugin, disconnect, and connect to LinkedIn again to restore it.</p>
</div>
