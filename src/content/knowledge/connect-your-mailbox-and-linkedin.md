  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">User Settings</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: User Settings in the profile menu">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/0a065613-fb81-415d-abc4-0d3b385b455c/user_cropped_screenshot_aced51cc9f614b9abb809bc6f9aae34a_text_export.jpeg" alt="User Settings in the profile menu" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />

  <li>

    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: the Mailbox tab">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/dacb61e8-a54b-42b1-8e0f-6c6867152c78/user_cropped_screenshot_6fb30c00a6e44210befad9530aa2c2e7_text_export.jpeg" alt="The Mailbox tab under User Settings" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>The Mailbox tab</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Select your email provider. For a Google account, click <span class="kb-kbd">Sign in with Google</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: choosing the email provider">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/dfe455d1-9e9d-43ec-a74d-475a45887c67/user_cropped_screenshot_e6ce763819f74e19a66160493ced721d_text_export.jpeg" alt="Choosing an email provider with Sign in with Google" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />

<h2 class="kb-sub" id="linkedin">Connect LinkedIn with the browser plugin</h2>
<ol class="kb-steps" start="8">
  <li>
    <p>Still in <span class="kb-kbd">User Settings</span>, select the <span class="kb-kbd">LinkedIn</span> tab.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: the LinkedIn tab">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/cfc936fd-f8cf-404f-aeee-f2de60e9b742/user_cropped_screenshot_5a377431141c4db6bc7a45ec17102da5_text_export.jpeg" alt="The LinkedIn tab under User Settings" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>The LinkedIn tab</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Download Google Chrome extension</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: the extension download link">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/ba7c4862-dfd4-4fe9-8ae4-fdbea394d16e/user_cropped_screenshot_0b307d5afca54c0e936dd742d966b35f_text_export.jpeg" alt="The Download Google Chrome extension link" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Downloading the extension</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Add to Chrome</span> in the Chrome Web Store.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: Add to Chrome in the Web Store">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/76a69227-ddc7-43cf-8e1a-899f053eb9b3/user_cropped_screenshot_f96089cfdd334dc88be5ebf0ae416b2e_text_export.jpeg" alt="Add to Chrome in the Chrome Web Store" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Adding the extension</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Add extension</span> to confirm.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: confirming the install">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/1ae1cd2f-d949-424d-9853-1e649703d2ff/user_cropped_screenshot_74b6e68ae8464065af76851aa6c0e116_text_export.jpeg" alt="Confirming the extension install" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />

    </figure>

  <li>
    <p>Open the hubsell plugin from the Extensions menu in your browser.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: opening the plugin from Extensions">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/7bbaef07-2580-4c44-8749-6829748534a3/user_cropped_screenshot_08aed394674b4bdd8e50f80f172ea255_text_export.jpeg" alt="Opening the hubsell plugin from the Extensions menu" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />

    <p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Connect to LinkedIn</span> in the plugin pop-up.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: Connect to LinkedIn in the plugin">

    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: refreshing the hubsell page">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/6a3a6639-2938-4dc6-b33c-cd144d28ce0b/user_cropped_screenshot_9405efb68b0d4ebca89fdbe6c026c9ae_text_export.jpeg" alt="Refreshing the hubsell page after connecting" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Refreshing to pick up the new status</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
</ol>
