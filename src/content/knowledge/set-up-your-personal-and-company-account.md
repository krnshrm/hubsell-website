<h2 class="kb-sub">Complete your personal details</h2>
<ol class="kb-steps">
  <li><p>In the left sidebar, click your name at the bottom.</p></li>
  <li>
    <p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">User Settings</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: account menu with User Settings highlighted">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-14/1a99ece2-bed3-4227-92cc-c9648b6d6cb1/ascreenshot_822bf8e1a43c427ebbe9ae762ab1dc2b_text_export.jpeg" alt="The account menu open in the left sidebar with User Settings highlighted" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Opening User Settings from the sidebar</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Fill in your personal information in the form.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: personal information form">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-14/9e9aedbf-d41b-4802-adc4-e3d60bd78769/ascreenshot_56db3c20973f41b9815e15ace6789ffa_text_export.jpeg" alt="The personal information form under User Settings" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>The personal information form</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li><p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Save changes</span>.</p></li>
</ol>

<h2 class="kb-sub">Complete your company details</h2>
<ol class="kb-steps" start="5">
  <li><p>In the left sidebar, click your name again.</p></li>
  <li>
    <p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Company Settings</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: account menu with Company Settings highlighted">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-14/c515c5f6-9f2d-46d9-835e-4a1fa8e59fbc/ascreenshot_7f4ab47524bf47fd96e6dd827117f674_text_export.jpeg" alt="The account menu with Company Settings highlighted" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Opening Company Settings from the sidebar</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Fill in the fields under the <b>Company information</b> tab.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: Company information tab">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-14/1c6242e8-7f19-47e8-ac75-63c4813f6ef8/ascreenshot_5425a7454ecf490e862016023ad8d825_text_export.jpeg" alt="The Company information tab with its form fields" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>The Company information tab</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li><p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Save changes</span>.</p></li>
</ol>
