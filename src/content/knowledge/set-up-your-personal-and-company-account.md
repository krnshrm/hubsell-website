<p>Your account details identify you across hubsell and show up in parts of your outreach, so filling them in now keeps your later campaigns looking right.</p>

<h2 class="kb-sub">Complete your user details</h2>
<ol class="kb-steps">
  <li>
    <p>Click your username at the bottom of the left sidebar.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: username at the bottom of the left sidebar">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/ffc7ffc7-e9f9-4676-83bb-a83b24c6854f/user_cropped_screenshot_eb5cd979469e4745b4af9754bf242a33_text_export.jpeg" alt="The username at the bottom of the left sidebar" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Your username at the bottom of the sidebar</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">User Settings</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: User Settings in the profile menu">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/4cf9a330-74ab-49e1-b122-4d38de958b5b/user_cropped_screenshot_7c34d9ddb04048c8ac61aa3d1b7e8aa1_text_export.jpeg" alt="User Settings in the profile menu" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Opening User Settings</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Fill in your personal details in the form fields.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: the personal details form">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/3f3b73fc-dd66-4684-9781-42dcf7b77b13/user_cropped_screenshot_2bb5478de7fc4eefbd57e86cca3884e2_text_export.jpeg" alt="The personal details form under User Settings" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>The personal details form</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Click <span class="kb-kbd">Save changes</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: Save changes on User Settings">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/fa47eb24-d3c6-4ba1-8f10-0fba7f329865/user_cropped_screenshot_f03da5f9d5874c67b7a6bd286b397940_text_export.jpeg" alt="The Save changes button on User Settings" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
