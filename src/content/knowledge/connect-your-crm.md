<div class="kb-callout kb-callout--tip">
  <p>Supported CRMs are <b>HubSpot</b>, <b>Salesforce</b> and <b>Pipedrive</b>. Once connected, the sync runs in real time.</p>
</div>

<h2 class="kb-sub">Connect your CRM</h2>
<ol class="kb-steps">
  <li>
    <p>Click the profile menu in the bottom left corner and select <span class="kb-kbd">Integrations</span>.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: Integrations in the profile menu">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/01204e02-78d7-476e-a35b-25519b35c52d/user_cropped_screenshot_07032bae7dec4bd096500a11a3d76c08_text_export.jpeg" alt="Integrations in the profile menu" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Opening Integrations</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Select the CRM you want to connect.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: choosing a CRM to connect">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/f2e55ff7-e65a-49c2-94a3-b87b7f7e2c12/user_cropped_screenshot_3616d1c85b804774b6d5a8eda22a60c8_text_export.jpeg" alt="Choosing which CRM to connect" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Choosing your CRM</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>hubsell sends you to your CRM. Grant the permissions it asks for to authorise the connection.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: granting permissions in your CRM">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/48b14867-ca73-4878-8e63-64e7ed96f9dc/user_cropped_screenshot_9ed791cc2001431684fa7a990b42cd0d_text_export.jpeg" alt="Granting permissions in your CRM to authorise the connection" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Authorising the connection</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
  <li>
    <p>Go back to hubsell and refresh the <span class="kb-kbd">Integrations</span> page.</p>
    <figure class="kb-figure" data-ph="Screenshot: the CRM showing as connected">
      <img src="https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/c7d75092-b837-43cd-aca4-fba1b1e76cb7/screenshot_c6881b7369fb4e8785c6a0df8c1e049b_text_export.jpeg" alt="The Integrations page showing the CRM as connected" loading="lazy" onerror="this.closest('.kb-figure').classList.add('is-missing')" />
      <figcaption>Your CRM connected</figcaption>
    </figure>
  </li>
</ol>

<div class="kb-callout kb-callout--warn">
  <p class="kb-callout__h">Troubleshooting</p>
  <ul>
    <li><b>Contacts are not appearing in your CRM.</b> Pushing contacts to the CRM is a manual action. Connecting the account does not push anything by itself, you have to trigger it.</li>
  </ul>
