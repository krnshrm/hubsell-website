<h2 class="kb-sub">What enrichment does</h2>
<p>A raw contact list is often incomplete. You might have a name and a company but no verified email, or a LinkedIn profile with no direct contact details. Enrichment fills those gaps. It adds the missing pieces to each record so a contact is ready to reach out to, rather than a partial row you cannot act on. For a plain definition of the term, see the <a href="/glossary/data-enrichment">data enrichment glossary entry</a>.</p>

<h2 class="kb-sub">Where you choose it</h2>
<p>You pick an enrichment option during import, whether you source from LinkedIn, Sales Navigator, or a CSV file. Choose based on how complete you need each record to be before it enters a campaign. The exact options appear in the import flow, covered step by step in <a href="/knowledge/sourcing-data-into-hubsell">Source data into hubsell</a>.</p>
