By the end of this article your CRM will be connected to hubsell, so you can push contacts across from hubsell when you are ready.

**Prerequisites:** a CRM account with permission to authorise a new integration.

> Supported CRMs are HubSpot, Salesforce and Pipedrive. Once connected, the sync runs in real time.

## Steps

1. Click the profile menu in the bottom left corner and select **Integrations**.

   ![Integrations in the profile menu](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/01204e02-78d7-476e-a35b-25519b35c52d/user_cropped_screenshot_07032bae7dec4bd096500a11a3d76c08_text_export.jpeg)

2. Select the CRM you want to connect.

   ![CRM options on the integrations page](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/f2e55ff7-e65a-49c2-94a3-b87b7f7e2c12/user_cropped_screenshot_3616d1c85b804774b6d5a8eda22a60c8_text_export.jpeg)

3. hubsell sends you to your CRM. Grant the permissions it asks for to authorise the connection.

   ![CRM permission screen](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/48b14867-ca73-4878-8e63-64e7ed96f9dc/user_cropped_screenshot_9ed791cc2001431684fa7a990b42cd0d_text_export.jpeg)

4. Go back to hubsell and refresh the **Integrations** page.

   ![Integrations page showing the CRM as connected](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/c7d75092-b837-43cd-aca4-fba1b1e76cb7/screenshot_c6881b7369fb4e8785c6a0df8c1e049b_text_export.jpeg)

## What you should see

The **Integrations** page shows your CRM as connected. From this point the sync runs in real time, but contacts are not sent across on their own. You choose when to push them.

## Troubleshooting

- **Contacts are not appearing in your CRM.** Pushing contacts to the CRM is a manual action. Connecting the account does not push anything by itself, you have to trigger it.
- **The Integrations page still shows the CRM as not connected.** Refresh the page. The status only updates on reload.
