By the end of this article your personal profile and your company profile in hubsell will both be complete.

**Prerequisites:** a hubsell account you can log in to at app.hubsell.com.

## Complete your user details

1. Click your username at the bottom of the left sidebar.

   ![Username at the bottom of the left sidebar](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/ffc7ffc7-e9f9-4676-83bb-a83b24c6854f/user_cropped_screenshot_eb5cd979469e4745b4af9754bf242a33_text_export.jpeg)

2. Click **User Settings**.

   ![User Settings in the profile menu](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/4cf9a330-74ab-49e1-b122-4d38de958b5b/user_cropped_screenshot_7c34d9ddb04048c8ac61aa3d1b7e8aa1_text_export.jpeg)

3. Fill in your personal details in the form fields.

   ![Personal details form](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/3f3b73fc-dd66-4684-9781-42dcf7b77b13/user_cropped_screenshot_2bb5478de7fc4eefbd57e86cca3884e2_text_export.jpeg)

4. Click **Save changes**.

   ![Save changes button on user settings](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/fa47eb24-d3c6-4ba1-8f10-0fba7f329865/user_cropped_screenshot_f03da5f9d5874c67b7a6bd286b397940_text_export.jpeg)

## Complete your company details

5. Click your username at the bottom of the left sidebar again.

   ![Username at the bottom of the left sidebar](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/33ac19fd-36fe-47df-8913-206c61b68714/user_cropped_screenshot_9cea9b72c325468ca2a49b89cd40b5d2_text_export.jpeg)

6. Click **Company Settings**.

   ![Company Settings in the profile menu](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/bd067e0c-873d-4eb8-b7d0-82aea9cc768d/user_cropped_screenshot_818be0194e79402f938739aa4a0940f4_text_export.jpeg)

7. Fill in the fields on the **Company information** tab.

   ![Company information tab](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/0e032b23-8cb7-42dc-bd13-328aff6acfd5/user_cropped_screenshot_660c9014b95c4f41af32459316921b30_text_export.jpeg)

8. Click **Save changes**.

   ![Save changes button on company settings](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/01db6f9c-4601-498b-abc3-9a74d3dd5df4/user_cropped_screenshot_dfb3cb7191894e91a5c71292b497a8ee_text_export.jpeg)

## What you should see

Both settings screens keep the values you entered. Reopen **User Settings** and **Company Settings** and your details are still there. Your account is now ready for the next step, connecting your mailbox and LinkedIn.
