By the end of this article your mailbox and your LinkedIn account will both be connected to hubsell, so it can send email and run LinkedIn actions for you.

**Prerequisites:** a hubsell account you can log in to, a Google or Microsoft mailbox, and Google Chrome for the LinkedIn plugin.

> Supported mailbox providers are Google and Microsoft. The steps below show the Google flow. Microsoft follows the same path, with the Microsoft sign in screen instead.

## Connect your mailbox

1. Click your profile name at the bottom of the left sidebar.

   ![Profile name at the bottom of the left sidebar](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/e6488cf7-27d5-4326-b03e-244004ba095d/user_cropped_screenshot_82eedf20c0564cab8eb2058ce38bee7e_text_export.jpeg)

2. Click **User Settings**.

   ![User Settings in the profile menu](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/0a065613-fb81-415d-abc4-0d3b385b455c/user_cropped_screenshot_aced51cc9f614b9abb809bc6f9aae34a_text_export.jpeg)

3. Select the **Mailbox** tab.

   ![Mailbox tab in user settings](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/dacb61e8-a54b-42b1-8e0f-6c6867152c78/user_cropped_screenshot_6fb30c00a6e44210befad9530aa2c2e7_text_export.jpeg)

4. Select your email provider. For a Google account, click **Sign in with Google**.

   ![Email provider selection](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/dfe455d1-9e9d-43ec-a74d-475a45887c67/user_cropped_screenshot_e6ce763819f74e19a66160493ced721d_text_export.jpeg)

5. Select the Google account you want to connect.

   ![Google account picker](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-22/fd6db62a-e57b-442c-b528-9e61c4429e1e/user_cropped_screenshot_2f3343bb93804eda9ae53c6e05916d0c_text_export.jpeg)

6. Click **Continue**.

   ![Continue on the Google consent screen](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/7dc626f3-c8dc-4ec6-b91a-68e0ccc5bdcf/user_cropped_screenshot_7563b890110e4d49879296c567f2c40b_text_export.jpeg)

7. Click **Allow** to grant the permissions hubsell needs.

   ![Allow permissions](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/45f34266-d677-4242-85e5-f823d83d6e09/user_cropped_screenshot_79b53168fdab4448b2f17ddebb9c06c6_text_export.jpeg)

## Connect LinkedIn with the browser plugin

8. Still in **User Settings**, select the **LinkedIn** tab.

   ![LinkedIn tab in user settings](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/cfc936fd-f8cf-404f-aeee-f2de60e9b742/user_cropped_screenshot_5a377431141c4db6bc7a45ec17102da5_text_export.jpeg)

9. Click **Download Google Chrome extension**.

   ![Download Chrome extension button](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/ba7c4862-dfd4-4fe9-8ae4-fdbea394d16e/user_cropped_screenshot_0b307d5afca54c0e936dd742d966b35f_text_export.jpeg)

10. Click **Add to Chrome** in the Chrome Web Store.

    ![Add to Chrome in the web store](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/76a69227-ddc7-43cf-8e1a-899f053eb9b3/user_cropped_screenshot_f96089cfdd334dc88be5ebf0ae416b2e_text_export.jpeg)

11. Click **Add extension** to confirm.

    ![Add extension confirmation](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/1ae1cd2f-d949-424d-9853-1e649703d2ff/user_cropped_screenshot_74b6e68ae8464065af76851aa6c0e116_text_export.jpeg)

12. Open the hubsell plugin from the Extensions menu in your browser.

    ![hubsell plugin in the extensions menu](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/7bbaef07-2580-4c44-8749-6829748534a3/user_cropped_screenshot_08aed394674b4bdd8e50f80f172ea255_text_export.jpeg)

13. Click **Connect to LinkedIn** in the plugin pop-up.

    ![Connect to LinkedIn in the plugin](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/acac30f3-39ce-4f87-8aeb-a441ee9de5ca/user_cropped_screenshot_458e7cf5043643c78c556e35632e5ddf_text_export.jpeg)

14. Refresh the hubsell page.

    ![Refreshed hubsell page](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/6a3a6639-2938-4dc6-b33c-cd144d28ce0b/user_cropped_screenshot_9405efb68b0d4ebca89fdbe6c026c9ae_text_export.jpeg)

## What you should see

The **Mailbox** tab shows your connected email address, and the **LinkedIn** tab shows LinkedIn as connected. Once both are in place hubsell can send email and run LinkedIn actions on your behalf.

![LinkedIn shown as connected](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2026-07-20/95ee807f-433e-4759-b889-c017ed8cfacc/user_cropped_screenshot_0b346ce76a2b4d0daca1b46d67791c85_text_export.jpeg)

## Troubleshooting

- **LinkedIn still shows as not connected after you click Connect to LinkedIn.** Refresh the hubsell page. The status only updates on reload.
- **A search later gets stuck on Paused.** This usually means the LinkedIn session has gone out of sync. Disconnect and reconnect LinkedIn from the hubsell plugin.
